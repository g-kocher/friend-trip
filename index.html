Methodology & assumptions

  • Pricing proxy: 2027 summer fares and lodging rates aren't bookable yet (airlines typically open schedules ~11 months out). Every figure here uses current comparable-season pricing for the same routes/properties as a stand-in — treat as directional, and expect to re-price 3–6 months before the trip.
  • Trip window used for costing: 4 nights, representative dates Thu Jul 15 – Mon Jul 19, 2027 (the group has flexibility from mid-June through mid-July).
  • Fee/tax consistency: most destinations' lodging figures include local occupancy tax and cleaning fees; Las Vegas and a couple of others explicitly exclude them (noted per destination) — actual gap could run 10–25% higher there.
  • Not included anywhere: food, activities/tickets, trip insurance, and rental cars except where a destination specifically requires one (noted).
  • Each family priced from its highest-preference airport unless a lower-preference airport was meaningfully cheaper or better-routed — flagged in that destination's detail card when relevant.
